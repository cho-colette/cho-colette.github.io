Crate

Structure

To neutralize a possible explosion, we propose a 4-hemisphere solution.

A spherical inter-latching shell without any small parts, such as screws, includes a physical structure to reduce the effects of shock-waves, such as bullet-proof (kevlar) or nano-enhanced (porous copper) sheets. One half of each hemisphere goes fully into a half of the next part, each part supplying the action-reaction, keeping the whole mechanism intact. In order to take care of the released heat, pressure, and burnt chemicals if an energetic material does go off inside our crate, we want to institute a layered structure:

A thermoelectric layer will convert the released heat, covered by a piezoelectric layer to convert mechanical stress, into electricity and charge a built-in battery. An outer filter-layer will clean the air released to the surroundings. Another sound-insulating layer will remove the noise by minimizing the amplitude of the released acoustic waves. This way, we can charge our phones to tweet to the media about how we are unharmed and ready to defend ourselves!

Energetic Materials

Crate is equipped for containing:

  • Explosives
  • Pyrotechnic compositions
  • Propellants
  • Fuels
  • Batteries (Li or Pb-acid)
